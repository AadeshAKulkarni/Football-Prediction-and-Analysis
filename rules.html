Daily 11

  • A day with 1 match: Minimum 5 players from each team
  • A day with 2 matches: Minimum 2 players from each team
  • Squad freezes 15 minutes before the match begins

Tournament 11

  • Maximum 3 players from each team.
  • Total 12 substitutions for the complete duration of the tournament
  • Squad freezes 15 minutes before the match begins

All Star 11

  • 1 player from each of the 10 teams in the Second/Super 10 round
  • 11th player can be from any of the 10 teams
  • One-time squad, no substitutions allowed
  • Squad freezes 15 minutes before the match begins
