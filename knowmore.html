Daily 11

In Daily 11, you can select your players every day from the teams that are playing a match on that day. The team selection will close 15 minutes prior to the first match that commences on that day. Based on the players selected, your scores will be updated daily after 3 hours of the match completion.

If you are a cricket devotee and regularly follow all the matches of the T-20 Tournament, Daily 11 is the format for you. Click to register here for daily 11.

Make sure you have read the Rules and Terms and Conditions pages so that you understand the game format in greater detail.

Tournament 11

In tournament 11, you can select a team of 11 only once during the entire season. In addition, you will be allowed to make 12 substitutions throughout the period.

We have included this, for all the busy students and professionals who may not be able to update their team, on a daily basis. So, if you're travelling for work or busy with exams, this is the right option for you. Click to register here for tournament 11.

All Star 11

In All Star 11, you can create the team only once during the entire season with no substitutions allowed.

If you think you know the players who would come out as stars of the tournament (like they say, lambi race ka ghoda) and if you want to place your bet only on them, All Star 11 is the format for you. Click to register here for All Star 11.

League 11

A league enables you to invite your friends, family and colleagues and track their performance and compete with them. You can make a league; join a league or search for a league. Click Make a league to get started now. You can form leagues in only Daily 11 format.

One person can be a part of multiple leagues.

Step 1

Get your friends and family to join this competitive game. Stay connected with them and track each other's score as the top 3 leagues are entitled to exciting prizes!

Step 2

Invite Friends and family to join your league. You can invite users who are already registered and also invite others who have not registered on the site yet.

Step 3

Check your team's score after the match to see the points your league has scored.
