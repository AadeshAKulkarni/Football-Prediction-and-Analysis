About FootPred

FootPred is an online fantasy football game that tests your football knowledge and love for the game. You get a chance to predict and bet points on various statistics of a BPL match. You earns points on basis of the right predictions and the odds.

Sounds interesting, doesn’t it? There's more to it. At FootPred, we have multiple types of predictions; depending upon the time and the love you have for the game. There are daily and seasonal predictions, as well as predictions for important statistics of the game, thus increasing your chances to win more points on more predictions.


So what are you waiting for? Don't just be a spectator and participate in the FootPred fantasy game.
